Projects

The following page will include a description of projects I have completed, including an Elevator Pitch, a single page web site with form processing and a case study about copyright infringements.

An Elevator Pitch Video

This was a 30 second video I made to demonstrate how to perform an elevator pitch, which emulates meeting a potential employer on an elevator and describing why they should hire you within 30 seconds or less which is the average time of an elevator ride.

This project helped me practice my communication skills as well as helped me develope video production and editing skills.

HTML Final Assignment Website

This was a final project for my HTML/CSS course in my first semester which brought together all the skills I acquired durring the course. It involved creating a web form and sending the information from that form to an external page to be processed.

Court Case Summary

This was a project which involved reading through the proceedings of a trademark registration court case involving the opponent Bell Mobility Inc and the applicant Validas LLC. The goal was to summerize in plain terms the court level, arguments and evidence from each party and to explain what the resulting ruling by the judges was. This project helped strengthen my understanding of the legal system in regards to copyright infringements in an online setting.
