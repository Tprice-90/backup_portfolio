Employment History

Most of my employment history includes kitchen work but I have also had experience in other industries such as Auto Recycling General Labourer, Mould Making, and Technical Support. The following will list each of the positions I've held and a brief description of the duties I was responsible for

BOS Auto Parts - General Labour(Summer - 2006)

As a general Labourer, my duties included organizing and taking inventory of automobile parts, removing tires from rims, loading unsuable parts to be scrapped, general cleaning and lawn maintenance.

The Michigan Diner - Dishwasher(2006 - 2007)

As a dishwasher, duties included keeping dishes stocked for both cooks and wait staff, assisting cooks durring rush times when needed, assisting prep cook, general cleaning.

Red Lobster - Dishwasher/Fry Cook(2007 - 2012)

As a dishwasher, duties inlcluded keeping dishes stocked for cooks and wait staff, assisting prep cooks in their duties, unloading new product from truck, keeping fridges and freezers organized. As a fry cook, duties included operating fryers, organizing and maintaining fryer station and fridge.

Sutherland Global Services - Technical Support(2012)

As technical support for Blackberry Mobile department I assisted customers in troubleshooting issues regarding the Blackberry Playbook device. My duties included keeping records of customer names and information, as well as troubleshooting steps taken for further inquiries, reporting tickets for issues that were not resolved and following up with customers on their issues.

Concours Technologies - Apprentice Mould Maker(2012 - 2013)

Main position to operate the Electrical Dischard Machine(EDM) which had me performing cuts on various types of metal to precisely resize moulds to be used on other projects throughout the shop. Also used ban saw to size pieces of carbon which were moulded to create cutting tools for other machines.

Dominion Golf Course and Country Club - Dishwasher/Cook(2013 - present)

Began as a dishwasher and prep cook, duties included keeping dishes stocked for cooks and wait staff, emptying used fryer grease to outdoor containers, preparing various foods to be used durring service, assisting cooks durring rush times. As a cook, duties included assigning tasks to other cooks and dishwashers, taking inventory of foods and other products, placing orders for food stock, designing daily specials, maintaining cooking equipment.

Hobbies

Some of my hobbies include gaming and music

Gaming

For most of my life I have been a huge fan of gaming on all platforms from the original Nintendo to Xbox One. It has been a leading factor in my schooling path because earlier on in my life I wanted to design video games that millions of people would enjoy. This later evolved into a more general web developement path when I decided that game developement itself was not the exact career I wanted to get into.

In this section I will go over some of my favourite games from throughout the years.

Super Mario Bros. 3

My very first memory in gaming was being at my nana's house playing Super Mario Bros. 3. I consider this game as one of the best video games of all time. While, I played the other 2 games in this series, this was the first time I remember putting all my effort into not only completing the game, but finding all the different secrets and areas there were to explore. To this day I sometimes go back to playing it just for the nostalgia that it brings to me.

World of Warcraft

This was my first entry into Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing gaming. WoW is a true classic when it comes to MMORPGs because it allows you to truly play a role and immerse yourself in a massive world to explore. While the current oppinion of the game is that it has been watered down content-wise, I have many good memories of logging on with my school friends and going on quests, leveling up and just generally having a really good time.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Skyrim was my first entry in the Elder Scrolls series of games, and to this day I still consider it a masterpiece in terms of Role Playing Games. This game, like World Of Warcraft allowed you to immerse yourself into a world that felt real. Playing as a weapon-weilding Warrior, sneaky Thief, spell slinging Mage, or anything in between, Skyrim was a massive world that I spent hours exploring.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is one of my favourite series in the First Person Shooter genre of gaming. There are many titles in this series that I have played but my favourite has to be a tie between Black Ops 1 and Black Ops 2. From the very well designed Campaign mode(Story) to the dark and high stress Zombies mode, Call Of Duty is another title that I spent countless hours with friends playing.

Music

Music has always been an important part of my life. I use music to de-stress, get excited, and help me better understand how I am feeling. I also consider myself a novice Musician, as I do play guitar and bass. I have also had some experience drumming but never practiced enough to consider myself proficient.

My current guitars include; a Jay Turser Purple Serpent which is a Gibson Les Paul style guitar which holds a lot of meaning to me. I have used it to perform in a handful of live shows.

My other guitar is an acoustic Epiphone which is the main guitar I use around the house when I am inspired to play.

Other instuments that I have experience with are Trumpet and Flute but I only played these in school.
